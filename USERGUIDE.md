# BrewLedger — User Guide

## What is BrewLedger?

BrewLedger is a native macOS app for tracking your home coffee brewing. It stores everything locally on your Mac — no cloud, no accounts, no tracking.

## Quick Start

1. **Launch BrewLedger** from your Applications folder.
2. The **Dashboard** tab shows your recent brews and bean count.
3. To log your first brew, go to the **Brews** tab and click **+**.
4. To add beans to your inventory, go to the **Beans** tab and click **+**.

## Features

### Log a Brew

- Go to **Brews** tab → click **+** (top-right)
- Fill in: Bean Name, Grind Size, Water Temp (°C), Brew Method, Rating (1-5 stars), optional Notes
- Click **Save**

### Browse & Search Brews

- **Brews** tab lists all brews, newest first
- Type in the search bar to filter by bean name, brew method, or rating
- Click any brew to see full details

### Manage Bean Inventory

- **Beans** tab lists all beans with roast date and remaining amount (grams)
- Click **+** to add a new bean
- Right-click a bean → **Delete** to remove it

### Delete a Brew

- Right-click any brew in the list → **Delete**
- Or open the brew detail and click the trash icon in the toolbar

### Settings

- **Settings** tab lets you set a default brew method (auto-selected when logging)
- Click **Export Brews as JSON** to save your brew history to a file

## Data & Privacy

- All data stored locally in your Mac's Application Support folder
- No internet connection required
- No cloud sync, no accounts, no analytics

## Export Your Data

1. Go to **Settings** → **Export Brews as JSON**
2. Choose where to save the file
3. Your brew history is exported as a readable JSON file

## Requirements

- macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later
- Apple Silicon or Intel Mac
